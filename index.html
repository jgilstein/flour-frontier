Where the next ten years of NAF FSI flour growth lives

My summer internship project at General Mills set out to answer one question: where should NAF FSI focus flour milling and warehouse capacity over the next ten years. I built the analysis with a lot of Tableau and Circana data, Census projections, distributor invoice records, and conversations with the Account Executives who actually own the territories.
