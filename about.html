About Jake

I am Jake Gilstein, a North America Foodservice Sales Intern at General Mills in the Summer 2026 cohort. I am a senior at the University of South Florida graduating December 2026 with a B.S. in Business Management and a Minor in Marketing. Born in Brooklyn, NY. Raised in Philadelphia, PA. Now splitting time between St. Augustine, FL and Tampa, FL. In my free time, I enjoy weightlifting, boxing, running, and listening to '70s-90's r&b & disco. I can almost always be spotted wearing one of three things: a quarter zip, a Knicks jersey or loafers.

My background blends two angles that turned out to be directly useful at General Mills. I spent the last 8 to 9 months at Scotlynn as a Logistics Account Manager Associate, brokering freight partnerships for major CPG clients, which gave me a working knowledge of how product physically gets from a plant to a customer. The summer before that I was a Strategic Sales Intern at The H.T. Hackney Co. in Tampa, doing 60 store visits per week analyzing buyer behavior and refining route-to-market strategy. The 3PL logistics work and the strategic CPG sales work both showed up directly in the Flour Frontier analysis.
