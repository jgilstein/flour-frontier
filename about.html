About Jake

I am Jake Gilstein, a North America Foodservice Sales Intern at General Mills in the Summer 2026 cohort. I am a senior at the University of South Florida graduating December 2026 with a B.S. in Business Management and a Minor in Marketing. Born in Brooklyn, NY. Raised in Philadelphia, PA. Now splitting time between St. Augustine, FL and Tampa, FL.

My background blends two angles that turned out to be directly useful at General Mills. The summer before that I was a Strategic Sales Intern at The H.T. Hackney Co. in Tampa, doing 60 store visits per week analyzing buyer behavior and refining route-to-market strategy. The 3PL logistics work and the strategic CPG sales work both showed up directly in the Flour Frontier analysis.
